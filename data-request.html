Request Your Data

Ask for a copy of the personal data Safer holds about you, or ask us to correct it.

You have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, request a copy, or ask us to correct inaccurate information. This page explains how.

What you can request

  • Access — a copy of the personal data associated with your account.
  • Correction — fix inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Portability — receive your data in a common, machine-readable format.
  • Deletion — see Delete Account.

How it works

  • Submit the form below or email support@safer.global from your account email.
  • We may ask you to verify your identity to protect your account.
  • We aim to respond within 30 days.
