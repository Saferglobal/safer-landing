The 4 things to try

How to use Safer.

Once you're set up, these are the moments Safer is built for.

SOS — your instant alarm

Press SOS when you feel unsafe. Your trusted contacts get an alert with your live location, right away.

Watch Me — hands-free

Turn it on when walking home or anywhere that feels off. Safer watches over you and can raise the alarm for you.

Trusted contacts

Choose who gets alerted. They'll get a message the moment you need help — so you're never really alone.

"I'm safe"

Once you're okay, tap I'm safe to let everyone know the alert is over and you're fine.

Please read this first

  • Tell your contacts you're testing. When you try SOS or Watch Me, real alerts go to real people — let them know first so they don't worry.
  • This is a test app, not a replacement for 911. In a real emergency, always call your local emergency number first.
  • It's early, so some things may be rough. That's exactly what you're helping us find. Thank you.
